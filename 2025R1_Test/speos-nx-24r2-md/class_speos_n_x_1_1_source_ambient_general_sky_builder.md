# Class SpeosNX::SourceAmbientGeneralSkyBuilder

![][Python]
![][public]

**Definition**: `SpeosNX.py` (line 13134)

Represents the builder for an Ambient Source with CIE Standard General Sky type.

The Ambient General Sky Source Builder is used to create and edit Ambient General Sky Source features.





This sky model is based on the publication of the CIE: ISO 15469:2004/CIE S 011:2003: Spatial distribution of daylight - CIE standard general sky.





To create a new instance of this class, use [FeatureCollection::CreateSourceAmbientGeneralSkyBuilder](.md#)

### Property CIEType

![][public]
![][static]

**Definition**: `SpeosNX.py` (line 13217)

```python
CIEType
```

Gets or sets the CIE type.

The values are:<br/>
 0 - CIE standard overcast sky.<br/>
 1 - Overcast with steep luminance gradation and slight brightening towards the sun.<br/>
 2 - Overcast, moderately graded with azimuthal uniformity.<br/>
 3 - Overcast, moderately graded and slight brightening towards the sun.<br/>
 4 - Sky of uniform luminance.<br/>
 5 - Partly cloudy sky, no gradation towards zenith, slight brightnening.<br/>
 6 - Partly cloudy sky, no gradation towards zenith, brighter circumsolar region.<br/>
 7 - Partly cloudy sky, no gradation towards zenith, distinct solar corona.<br/>
 8 - Partly cloudy sky, with the obscured sun.<br/>
 9 - Partly cloudy sky, with brighter circumsolar region.<br/>
 10 - White blue sky with distinct solar corona.<br/>
 11 - CIE standard clear sky, low luminance turbidity. <br/>
 12 - CIE standard clear sky, polluted atmosphere.<br/>
 13 - Cloudless turbid sky with broad solar corona.<br/>
 14 - White blue turbid sky with broad solar corona.<br/>






**Value type**: Integer.<br/>






The default value is 5.



**Return type**:
