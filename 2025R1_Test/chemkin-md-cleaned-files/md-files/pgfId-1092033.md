    SUBROUTINE CKAVG (NN, S1, S2, SAVG)
    For arrays of length nn,
    SAVG(n) is the average value of S1(n) and S2(n).

    INPUT
    NN    - The length of the two input arrays.
    S1    - Real array.
    S2    - Real array.

    OUTPUT
    SAVG  - Real array, sum of S1 and S2
    SAVG(n) is the average value of S1(n) and S2(n).
