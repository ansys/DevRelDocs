    SUBROUTINE CKCOPY (NN, X1, X2)
    Copy X1(*) array members into X2(*) array.
    
    INPUT
    NN        - Integer scalar; number of elements to copy.
    X1(*)     - Real array; dimension at least NN.
    
    OUTPUT
    X2(*)     - Real array; dimension at least NN.
