\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Define a time/\+frequency set. 

The time freq support describes an analysis\textquotesingle{} temporal or frequential space. For a transient analysis all the time sets cumulatives indices with their times are described. For a harmonic analysis, the real and imaginary frequencies, the RPMs, the load steps are described. \begin{Desc}
