### ConstraintType

```
enum ConstraintType 
```

Specifies constraint type 

|Enumerator|Description|
|----|----|
|Unconstrained  |No constraints |
|UpperAndLowerBound  |Constraints with upper and lower bounds|
|UpperBound  |Constraints with an upper but no lower bound |
|LowerBound  |Constraints with an lower but no upper bound |
|Unachievable  |Constraint that makes no runs feasible |

enum DesignVariableType

|Enumerator|Description|

|UpperAndLowerBound  |Design variables with upper and lower bounds |
|UpperBound  |Design variables with upper but no lower bound |
|LowerBound  |Design variables with lower but no upper bound |
